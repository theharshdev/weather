User Guide

Welcome to the Weather Website, your ultimate destination for accessing real-time weather updates, including temperature, humidity, and wind speed for any city or state globally. Simply type the name of your desired location in the search bar, and the website will promptly display the current weather data for that area. You can view the precise temperature, humidity levels, and wind speed to plan your day efficiently. Customize the settings to switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit for temperature and kilometers and miles for wind speed. Additionally, you can create a personalized account to save frequently visited locations, making it convenient to access weather updates quickly. For any queries or assistance, please reach out to our dedicated customer support team. Access the website seamlessly on your mobile devices to stay informed on the go. Thank you for choosing our Weather Website to stay updated and prepared for any weather conditions.
